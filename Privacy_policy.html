Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 30, 2024

Overact Games Privacy Policy

At Overact Games (“we” or “us”), we understand that privacy is important to our users. We are committed to protecting your personal information and handling it responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how Overact Games collects, uses, and shares information about you.

What Information Does Overact Games Collect?

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information You Provide to Us: This includes your name, email address, and other account details when you log in or create an account. Payment details for in-app purchases are handled by third-party providers, such as Unity In-App Purchases (IAP).
  • Information Automatically Collected: Technical data such as your device’s IP address, operating system, and device identifiers (e.g., advertising IDs) are collected for analytics and performance purposes.
  • Information from Third Parties: Unity Ads and Unity IAP may collect data to personalize ads and improve game features.

How Does Overact Games Use Your Information?

  • To enable gameplay features like login and leaderboards.
  • To deliver personalized advertisements through Unity Ads.
  • To securely process payments via Unity IAP or other gateways.
  • To send updates about game features, promotions, and other news.
  • To comply with legal obligations and ensure the security of our services.

Does Overact Games Share My Information?

We do not sell your information to third parties. However, we may share data in the following scenarios:

  • With service providers like Unity Ads and Unity IAP to process payments, show ads, and perform analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our terms.
  • In the event of a business transfer, such as a merger or sale of assets.

How Does Overact Games Protect My Information?

We use physical, technical, and administrative measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, so users should take steps to protect their accounts, like using strong passwords.

Does Overact Games Collect Information from Children?

Our services are intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents who believe their child has provided personal data should contact us immediately at geoscloud@gmail.com.

What Are My Privacy Choices?

  • Access or correct your personal data by contacting us.
  • Opt out of targeted advertising through your device or game settings.
  • Request deletion of your account or data by emailing us.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website, and significant updates will be communicated via in-game notifications or email.

Who Can I Contact with Concerns?

If you have questions or concerns about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: geoscloud@gmail.com
  • Email: overactgames@gmail.com
